Privacy Policy

How Traffic Guard handles your data.

Traffic Guard prioritizes privacy. We do not collect personally identifiable information from camera feeds. Only incident metadata (e.g., time, location, device ID) is stored. Federated learning ensures model parameters are trained locally; raw data is not uploaded.

Data We Store

  • Anonymous alert metadata: timestamp, device, location, and confidence.
  • Verification feedback (Verified/Rejected) without personal identifiers.
  • Aggregated model weights for federated learning.

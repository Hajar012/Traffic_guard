Awareness

Traffic safety awareness, data privacy, usage guidelines, and risk awareness.

Traffic Safety Awareness

  • Always follow traffic signals and speed limits.
  • Yield to pedestrians and emergency vehicles.
  • Maintain safe distance and avoid distractions.

Data Privacy

  • No personally identifiable information is stored in alerts.
  • Only metadata and model parameters are used for learning.
  • Federated learning keeps raw data on edge devices.

System Usage Guidelines

  • Guest users can view public information and summaries.
  • Authorities verify or reject alerts and export reports.
  • Admins manage users, devices, and model configurations.

Risk Awareness

  • AI detections may include false positives or negatives.
  • Verification decisions are logged for audit and improvement.
  • Use manual overrides carefully and follow policy.
